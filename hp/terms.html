1. Privacy Policy

If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy, please don't hesitate to contact us. At We Fix Solutions (WFS), the privacy of our visitors is of utmost importance to us. This privacy policy document highlight the types of personal information that WFS receives and collects, as well as how it is utilized.

For the purposes of this Privacy Notice, "Personal Information" refers to any information from or about an individual or a person, or where applicable, a household, that either directly identifies that individual or, where applicable, a household, or that makes them reasonably identifiable when combined with other information from or about that individual, or where applicable, a household, from any source.

We implement measures to safeguard the personal information of a person or an individual against loss, misuse, unauthorized access, unauthorized disclosure, manipulation, or destruction.

2. PERSONAL IDENTIFICATION INFORMATION

We may gather personal identification information from Users through various means, such as when Users visit our site, complete a form, or engage in other activities, services, features, or resources provided on our Site. Users may be requested to provide, as appropriate, their name, email address, and phone number. However, Users have the option to visit our Site anonymously. We will only collect personal identification information from Users if they willingly submit such information to us. Users can always choose not to disclose personally identifiable information, although doing so may restrict their participation in certain Site-related activities.

3. HOW WE USE COLLECTED INFORMATION

  • To improve customer service: The information Users provide aids us in responding to their customer service requests and support needs more effectively.

  • To personalize user experience: We may analyze information in the aggregate to comprehend how our Users, as a collective group, utilize the services and resources available on our Site.

  • To enhance our Site: Feedback provided by Users may be utilized to enhance our products and services.

  • To process payments: Information provided by Users when placing an order is used solely to fulfill that order. We do not share this information with external parties except as necessary to fulfill the service.

  • To conduct promotions, contests, surveys, or other Site features: We send Users information they agreed to receive about topics we believe will interest them.

  • To send periodic emails: The email address Users provide may be used to send them information and updates pertaining to their order. It may also be used to respond to their inquiries, questions, and/or other requests. If Users opt-in to our mailing list, they will receive emails containing company news, updates, related product or service information, etc. Users can unsubscribe from receiving future emails at any time using the unsubscribe instructions provided at the bottom of each email or by contacting us via our Site.

    • 4. HOW WE PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION

    We implement suitable data collection, storage, and processing practices along with security measures to safeguard against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of personal information, usernames, passwords, transaction information, and data stored on our Site.

    Sensitive and private data exchanges between the Site and Users occur over an SSL secured communication channel, ensuring encryption and protection with digital signatures. Additionally, our Site adheres to PCI vulnerability standards to establish a secure environment for Users.
